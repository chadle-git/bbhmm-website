We are thrilled to announce that the **BBHMM (Blockchain Better Have My Money)** stake pool is now live on the Cardano mainnet!

After months of preparation, testing, and infrastructure setup, we have officially launched our stake pool. Our mission is simple: provide a reliable, high-performance staking service for the Cardano community.

## What We Offer

- **Enterprise-grade infrastructure** hosted in a professional datacenter
- **1% variable fee** -- keeping more rewards in your pocket
- **24/7 monitoring** to ensure maximum uptime
- **Experienced operators** with deep knowledge of systems administration

## How to Delegate

Search for **BBHMM** in your favorite Cardano wallet (Yoroi, Daedalus, or Eternl) and delegate your ADA. Your funds remain in your control at all times -- staking on Cardano is fully non-custodial.

We look forward to producing blocks and earning rewards for our delegators. Welcome to the BBHMM family!
