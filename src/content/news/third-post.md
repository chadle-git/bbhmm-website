We are pleased to announce the completion of a **major infrastructure upgrade** for the BBHMM stake pool. This upgrade represents the most significant hardware refresh since our launch in 2020.

## What Changed

### New Hardware
- Upgraded to next-generation server platforms with higher core counts and faster clock speeds
- Installed new NVMe storage arrays for faster block processing and chain synchronization
- Expanded ECC memory for improved reliability and performance headroom

### Enhanced Monitoring
- Deployed a comprehensive monitoring stack with real-time alerting
- Added automated failover capabilities for improved resilience
- Implemented predictive health monitoring for proactive maintenance

### Network Improvements
- Upgraded to higher bandwidth network connections
- Added additional relay nodes for improved block propagation
- Enhanced DDoS mitigation capabilities

## Why This Matters

As the Cardano network grows and evolves, stake pool infrastructure must keep pace. This upgrade ensures that BBHMM can continue to produce blocks reliably and efficiently, maximizing rewards for our delegators.

The new hardware provides significant headroom for future protocol upgrades and increased network activity, positioning us well for the continued growth of the Cardano ecosystem.

## No Downtime

The upgrade was performed with zero downtime through a carefully planned rolling migration. Block production was maintained throughout the entire process.

Thank you to all our delegators for your continued trust in BBHMM. We remain committed to providing the best possible staking experience.
