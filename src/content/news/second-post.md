We are excited to announce our partnership with **TosiDrop**, the community-driven token distribution platform on Cardano!

## What This Means for Delegators

By staking with BBHMM, you now earn not just ADA staking rewards, but also additional token rewards distributed through the TosiDrop platform. This is a way for us to give back to our loyal delegators with extra value on top of standard Cardano rewards.

## How It Works

1. **Delegate to BBHMM** using your preferred Cardano wallet
2. **Visit TosiDrop** to check your eligible token rewards
3. **Claim your tokens** directly through the TosiDrop interface

No extra steps are needed beyond delegating your ADA to our pool. The TosiDrop platform handles the distribution automatically based on your delegation.

## About TosiDrop

TosiDrop is a token distribution platform built on Cardano that enables stake pool operators to distribute additional tokens to their delegators. It is a powerful tool for community building and delegator engagement.

Thank you to the TosiDrop team for this collaboration, and thank you to our delegators for your continued support!
